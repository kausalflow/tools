summary: Scholar Inbox is a personal paper recommender which enables researchers to stay up-to-date with the most relevant progress in their fields based on their personal research interests.

- path: scholar-inbox.png
features:
- Daily or weekly email alerts with your latest paper recommendations based on your trained AI model
- Vote for papers to improve your personal AI-based paper suggestions. Train your model by upvoting and downvoting papers
- Follow current research trends and find out which papers impressed other users in the community
- Create paper collections, export them as BibTeX, and get AI recommendations based on your curated collections
- Find papers via semantic search by providing text descriptions, or search by title, abstract, and authors
- Skim papers quickly with the help of AI-generated highlights and figure/table previews for faster comprehension
- Discover papers on an interactive visual map. Navigate by topic, zoom into research areas, and find relevant papers at a glance
- Explore conference schedules with interactive planners showing locations, paper relevance, and session details
